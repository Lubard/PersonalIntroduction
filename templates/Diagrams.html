Measure: Accuracy:
The percentage of questions in the category where the crowd forecast was on the correct side of 50%.

Brier Score:
A measure of the ‘error’ in a forecast that ranges between 0 and 2. A lower Brier Score is better; perfect forecasts have a Brier Score of 0, while forecasts that are no better than a random guess will have a Brier score of 0.5.
